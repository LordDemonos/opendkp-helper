Loot monitor exceptions

One rule per line. Matching loot is never captured, displayed, or posted to OpenDKP. Lines ending with : are prefix rules (e.g. Spell: ignores all spells). Other lines are exact item names (case-insensitive).

  • Spell: → ignores Spell: Blessing of Aegolism
  • A Glowing Orb of Luclinite → ignores that item only
